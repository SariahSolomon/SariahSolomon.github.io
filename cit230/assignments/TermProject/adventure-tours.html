Welcome to Bozeman Montana! There are so many incredible things to see in this corner of our country, and we want to help you see all of it, all from the comfort of your bicycle seat! We provide tours with a variety of difficulties and to a variety of sights in and around town. Check out our provided tours and then head over to our contact us page to set one up or request more information!

  • Advanced
    • Yellowstone Grand Tour: This 7-day biking trip begins at the Yellowstone entrance and will take you to all the best sights in Yellowstone. See geysers, waterfalls, and a variety of wildlife from the perspective of a lifelong local and wildlife expert. This tour takes place the last week of each month May-September and costs $500 per person.
    • Yellowstone and Back again: This 4-day biking trip begins in Bozeman and takes you down to the Yellowstone Entrance and back again. Cut through hills and valleys and keep an eye out for wildlife. This tour takes place every other week May-September and costs $350 per person
  • Moderate
    • A Night in Yellowstone: This 2-day biking trip begins at the Yellowstone Entrance and will take you around the Old Faithful loop. Camp in Canyon village and return with a set of new experiences and knowledge! This trip takes place Monday-Tuesday every week May-September and costs $300 per person
    • Three Forks Overnighter: This 2-day biking trip begins in Bozeman and takes you up to the Three Forks Campground before coming down the next morning. This trip takes place Friday-Saturday every week May-September and costs $300 per person
  • Beginner
    • Yellowstone Basics: This tour takes place on the Eleanor Lake Trail. Learn the basics of biking while enjoying scenic Yellowstone. Watch out for wildlife! This tour takes place Wednesdays and Fridays every week May-September and costs $200 per person
    • Welcome to Bozeman: Our version of a historical walking tour, this trip starts at our own Mountain Spoke tour and takes you to the historical sites around town. Learn about our foundations and roots, get tips and tricks for visiting Bozeman, and prepare for any potential trips to Yellowstone. This trip takes place Tuesdays and Thursdays every week April-October and costs $100 per person.
