Bike the Continental Divide

On July 27, 2017 forty-three adventures men and women set out on the adventure of a lifetime. They set out to bike the continental divide. Lined up at the trailhead in Glacier National Park, each biker was armed with a bike, helmet, and a determination to reach the end in New Mexico. Now it's your turn.
The Mountain Spoke's annual Bike the Continental Divide event will begin on August 3, 2018, and we want you to join us! This 3100 mile trail is not for the fainthearted, however. Each participant is required to have a bike pass our safety inspections, pass a qualification test themselves, and prove that they have all the required supplies to embark on this once-in-a-lifetime journey. Required supplies include:

  • Approved Bicycle
  • Helment
  • Quart-size or larger water bottle
  • Enough food for the trip
  • Tire pump
  • Extra inner tubes
  • Appropriate Cloting
  • Sleeping supplies(tent, sleeping bag, hammock, etc.)
  • Basic First Aid kit

Bike the Continental Divide will last approximately 2 weeks and costs $700 to enter. Don't want to do the whole thing? We have a number of shorter and easier options available as well. Interested? Fill out the form below and we'll get back to you as soon as possible with the details!
